Our Fire Extinguisher Services

At SafeGuard Fire Protection, we provide comprehensive fire extinguisher services to ensure your premises are fully protected and compliant with safety regulations.

ABC Powder Type

Multi-purpose dry chemical powder extinguisher for:

  • Class A (Wood, Paper, Textile)
  • Class B (Flammable Liquids)
  • Class C (Electrical Fires)
Commonly used in offices, homes, and commercial spaces.

DCP (Dry Chemical Powder)

Heavy-duty powder extinguisher for:

  • Industrial applications
  • Chemical fires
  • Metal fires (Class D)
Ideal for factories, workshops, and labs.

K Type

Wet Chemical extinguisher for:

  • Commercial kitchen fires
  • Cooking oil and fat fires
  • High-temperature equipment
Essential for restaurants and food facilities.

CO2 Type

Carbon dioxide extinguisher for:

  • Electrical equipment fires
  • Server rooms and data centers
  • Flammable liquid fires
Leaves no residue, perfect for sensitive equipment.

Mechanical Foam Type

Specialized foam-based extinguisher for:

  • Flammable liquid fires (Class B)
  • Oil and petroleum fires
  • Paint and solvent fires
Ideal for fuel stations, paint shops, and oil storage areas.

Water CO2 Type

Water-based pressurized extinguisher for:

  • Class A fires (Wood, Paper, Fabric)
  • General purpose fire fighting
  • Deep-seated fires
Perfect for warehouses, offices, and residential buildings.
